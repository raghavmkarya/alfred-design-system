# Alfred — voice & naming canon (design-relevant subset)

Verified against the live site 2026-07-01. Use these exact names and lines in any
asset built from this design system. Deeper canon: the product-marketing repo.

## Category & positioning

- Category: **decision intelligence platform** — never "AI chief of staff", never "BI tool".
- Homepage positioning (live): "AI Memory Powering Every Decision Across Your Organisation".
- Category definition (live FAQ, verbatim): decision intelligence "detects what changed,
  explains why, and recommends what to do next."
- Anti-positioning: "Not another dashboard, not another chatbot, not another analytics tool."
- Universal footer boilerplate (verbatim): "Alfred is the decision intelligence platform for
  marketing, sales, and the leaders running them. We connect your stack, synthesise what
  matters, and deliver decision-ready intelligence every day."
- Universal closing band (verbatim): "Built for the leaders who decide things. Marketing,
  sales, finance, operations, and the people running it all. Alfred is the intelligence
  layer underneath."

## Voice rules

- **Product, briefs, lifecycle email:** Alfred speaks first person — "I've flagged two
  reallocations…" — to "you". Lead with the decision or the number.
- **Marketing site:** third person about Alfred ("Alfred recommends…") or corporate "we".
- Sentence case everywhere; UPPERCASE only for small tracked eyebrows. No emoji, ever.
- No fabricated proof: stats carry a visible source or don't ship. The live 90+/$90M/90x
  band is a documented placeholder — never reuse those numbers as real proof.
- Best-in-class copy pattern (from the live product mocks): concrete, quantified, actionable —
  "Campaign burning $4.8K with zero conversions", "$55.1K/mo in recoverable waste".
